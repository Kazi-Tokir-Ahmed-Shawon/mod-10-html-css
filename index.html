BAT

Bats are fascinating creatures that belong to the order Chiroptera. They are the only mammals that are capable of sustained flight.




    Facts About Bats

  • Bats are the only mammals that can fly.
  • They play a crucial role as pollinators and seed dispersers.
  • There are over 1,400 species of bats worldwide.
  • Bats use echolocation to navigate and find prey.
  • Most bats have good eyesight, contrary to popular belief.
